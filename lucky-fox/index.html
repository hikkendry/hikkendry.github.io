Lucky Fox

Website and branding for these talented builders of handcrafted retail environments.

If you’re a bunch of talented creative retail designers you’re going to need a stand out identity and unique website. That’s why Lucky Fox came to us. After years building dazzling bespoke window installations and interiors for Levis, Harvey Nichols and Converse (to name but a few) Jason Traves decided to form his own retail design outfit and Lucky Fox was born.

As a man of impeccable taste with a keen eye for design we knew his branding and website needed to be clean and iconic. We delivered a carefully considered branding package and style guide that they’ve been able to apply in a range of creative ways from a customised ice-cream van to laser etched wood and even sticks of brighton rock!
