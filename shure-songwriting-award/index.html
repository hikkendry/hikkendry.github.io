Shure Songwriting Award

Website and intuitive CMS for the annual songwriting competition hosted by the worlds leading microphone manufacturer.

Let’s face it, making it big in the music industry is not easy. Every year, Shure (the leading manufacturer of microphones and headphones) and The ICMP (Europe’s leading independent school of modern music) host a songwriting competition for unsigned artists with great prizes that give entrants a foot in the music-biz door.

They approached us with an outdated flash site that desperately needed bringing up to date and integrating with their social platforms to increase reach and accessibility. Since taking on the project 2 years ago we’ve worked strategically to see entries increase and their Facebook audience more than quadruple.
